PizzaMate — Data Deletion & Privacy

How to request the deletion of your account and personal data.

Your right to deletion

You can request the permanent deletion of your PizzaMate account and the personal data associated with it. The data we store on our servers (Appwrite) and that will be removed includes:

  • alternate_email

    Account credentials

    Your email address and authentication record.

  • badge

    Profile information

    Your name, age, and gender.

  • photo_library

    Media

    Profile photos stored in our cloud storage.

How to request deletion

In-app account deletion is being finalized. In the meantime, you can request deletion manually by sending us an email.

  1. 1

    Send an email from your registered address

    Email us from the email address linked to your PizzaMate account. Requests from any other address cannot be verified.

  2. 2

    Use the subject line

    Data Deletion Request

  3. 3

    Send to our support address

    mail [abhishekverma998@gmail.com]

Processing time

We process verified data deletion requests within 7 business days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been removed from our systems.

warning

This action is permanent

Once your data deletion request is processed, your account, profile information, and uploaded photos cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account to use PizzaMate again.

For more details on what data we collect and how we use it, please review our Privacy Policy.
